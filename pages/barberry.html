Barberry

An endless runner game where Leo the Kangaroo jumps from barberry to barberry.

Overview

You may recognize Loxe as the pen-and-pencil game on children's menus in restaurants. Take turns making horizontal or vertical lines with your opponent. The player who completes the fourth side the most boxes wins!

Barberry was one of 5 iOS games used to generate money for children living in Bright Futures orphanage. See Rizikitoto for more details.
