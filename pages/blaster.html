Blaster

An Augmented Reality first-person shooter game.

Process

The inspiration for this game came from neopets, medieval game music, and The Adventures of Sharkboy and Lavagirl. My goal was to create a game that uses a multitude of environments without detracting from the overall color-popping theme. As such, I utilized clouds, grass fields, water, and skies as backgrounds for different scenes.
