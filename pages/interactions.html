Explorative Interactions

Interaction design concepts and techniques using Adobe After Effects.

Overview

AR Weather

This interaction allowed me to explore tracking objects within video footage, using 3D cameras with true depth of field to create pixel perfect compositions, and using split-tone color grade.

Pedometer Dashboard

This interaction allowed me to explore screen replacement within After Effects, using the camera solver to create a 3D camera, and transforming 3D scenes into objects.

Navigation Swipe

This micro-interaction allowed me to explore subtle 3D text animation and dig deep into faster animation workflows.

Navigation Expansion

This interaction allowed me to explore expressions, masks, and workflows to speed up the animation process.

Search

This micro-interaction allowed me to explore using track mattes, masks, and color overlays.

Textfield

This micro-interaction allowed me to practice timing and Photoshop integration in After Affects.

Tools: After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator
